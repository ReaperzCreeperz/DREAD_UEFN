S t o r y

The story follows Emily, a mute journalist investigating murders and disappearances.

Quiet and introverted, her silence heightens the tension.

She arrives at a cursed hotel on a rainy night, where a ritual unleashes darkness, forcing her into a horrifying ordeal.

Pursued by a masked, part-human, part-machine figure, Emily faces disturbing, grotesque environments and psychological terror, leading to a deadly confrontation that defines her fate.
